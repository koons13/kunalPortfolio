Ficus Notes

Rethinking How We Capture Thoughts

Managing notes often means endless folders, tags, and manual sorting. To solve this, I designed and launched a notes app that leverages AI to auto-categorise notes into folders, lists, and schedules—helping users stay organised without the extra effort.

With built-in intelligence, the app adapts to personal workflows, ensuring notes are always structured, searchable, and actionable.
