Baraka Poster

The task was to redesign the poster of 'Baraka', a 1992 non-narrative documentary film directed by Ron Fricke.

The documentary explores themes via a kaleidoscopic compilation of natural events, life, human activities and technological phenomena shot in 24 countries on six continents over a 14-month period. 

The poster is faced on the tribal ornaments shown in the film.

Guide: Prof. Nanki Nath, Department of Design, IIT Guwahati
